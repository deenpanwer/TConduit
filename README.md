# TRAC - Your AI-Powered Hiring Agent

TRAC is a platform that acts as your personal hiring agent, finding the best talent to solve your specific problems.

## How it works

1.  **Describe your problem:** Simply write down the challenge or task you need help with.
2.  **AI Agent gets to work:** Our intelligent agent scours the internet, analyzing profiles and skills to find the most suitable candidates.
3.  **Get matched:** We'll connect you with the right experts who can solve your problem efficiently.

Think of it as Google, but for finding people.
